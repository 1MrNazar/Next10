How It Works

Three steps to a more
intentional life

1

Define your goals

Set up to 510 long-term goals that actually matter to you. Lose weight. Save money. Learn a language. These become the anchors of your entire system.

2

Attach daily habits

Under each goal, define the concrete habits that move you toward it. "Run 3×/week" under "Lose weight." "30 min Duolingo" under "Learn a language." Small, trackable, repeatable.

3

Show up every day

Check off your habits. Watch your radar grow. Review the week. Repeat. The compound effect of small actions - made visible - is what builds a life you're proud of.

4

Stay connected to the vision

Next10 constantly reminds you of the big picture. Every habit you complete maps back to a goal. You never lose sight of why you're doing what you're doing.
